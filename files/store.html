Return & Exchange Policy

📦 Returns

We accept returns within 7 days of delivery. Items must be unused, in original packaging, with tags attached.

🔄 Exchanges

Size exchanges are accepted within 14 days. Contact us on WhatsApp with your order ID and the size you need.

🚫 Non-Returnable Items

Sale items, customized orders, and items showing signs of wear cannot be returned.

💸 Refunds

Approved refunds are processed within 5–7 business days to your original payment method.

📞 How to Initiate a Return

WhatsApp us your order ID and photos of the issue. We'll guide you through the process within 24 hours.
